Plex Arcade

Plex Arcade is a paid service that allows you to play classic Atari console games, plus your own collection of game ROMs and emulators on your most devices that can install the Plex client app.

View available games at: https://www.plex.tv/arcade/#modal-arcade-library

If you have your own game ROMs and emulators, you can add them to Plex to curate your own arcade.
