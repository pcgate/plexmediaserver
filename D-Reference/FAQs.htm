Is Plex the same as Netflix?

Plex provide free, ad-supported streaming of many movies and television shows. These broadcasts are available to everyone, without any cost, around the world.

Plex can't provide on-demand streaming the same way Netflix, Hulu, or similar services do.

Many people think of Plex as being like Netflix for their own content.

Content Provided by Plex includes:

Free, ad-supported Movies & Shows streaming service

Free streaming of more than 100 live TV

Web Shows and Podcasts
