About Plex

Plex began in 2007 as a freeware software project of developer Elan Feingold. Originally developed as a port of XMBC (Xbox Media Center, since renamed to Kodi) for the Mac operating system. Plex and it's server software are now available on all major operating systems and a range of devices such as TV's, set-top boxes and cellphones.
